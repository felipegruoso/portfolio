Privacy Policy

Effective Date: 2023-07-10

This Privacy Policy governs the manner in which we collect, use, maintain, and disclose personal data collected from users of our mobile game called "Sobrevivente". This Privacy Policy applies to the Game and all related services, features, and functionalities offered by us.

1. Personal Data Collection

   1.1. Device ID: We may collect device identification information, such as the unique device identifier (UDID) or other device-specific information. This data helps us identify and authenticate your device for the purpose of providing a personalized experience within the Game.

   1.2. Some Information: We collect additional information about your device, including, but not limited to, device model, operating system, device manufacturer, CPU architecture, memory information, storage information, battery level, connection type, locale and language settings, timezone, screen resolution, and app version. This information is collected by the monitoring tool Rollbar for error monitoring, performance improvements, and debugging purposes.

   1.3. Location: We may collect location data to enhance your gaming experience, provide personalized content, and offer location-based features within the Game.

   1.4. Player Name: We offer players the option to provide their name as a free text input. The player name is used solely for displaying your chosen name within the Game and associating it with your profile in the ranking system provided by LootLocker.

2. Use of Personal Data

   2.1. Ranking Feature: The personal data, including device ID and player name, is used to build and display rankings within the Game. This allows us to identify and associate your gameplay activity with your profile within the ranking system provided by LootLocker.

   2.2. Personalization: The personal data collected may be used to personalize your gaming experience, such as displaying your chosen name within the Game.

   2.3. External Tools: We may share personal data, including the device ID and player name, with external tools and service providers to facilitate the functioning of the ranking feature, improve the Game, and provide a seamless gaming experience. These external tools may include analytics providers, hosting services, or other third-party service providers. We ensure that these external tools comply with relevant data protection laws.

   2.4. Advertisements: Our Game may incorporate LevelPlay and UnityADS to display advertisements. We may collect certain personal data in accordance with its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review LevelPlay and Unity Technologies' privacy policy to understand how they handle your personal data.

3. Data Retention and Security

We retain the collected personal data for specific periods as outlined below:

   3.1. Device ID and Location: The device ID and location data are retained for the duration of your gameplay session and are not stored beyond that timeframe. This data is solely used during your active session to provide a personalized gaming experience and for displaying relevant advertisements through LevelPlay.

   3.2. Player Name: The player name, provided as a free text input, is stored securely in our database during your gameplay. It is used for the sole purpose of displaying your chosen name within the Game and associating it with your profile in the ranking system provided by LootLocker. We do not share or sell this information to any third parties.

   3.3. Monitoring Tools Data: The additional device information, such as device model, operating system, device manufacturer, CPU architecture, memory information, storage information, battery level, connection type, locale and language settings, timezone, screen resolution, and app version, used by Rollbar, is collected for error monitoring, performance improvements, and debugging purposes. This data is securely transmitted to the respective monitoring tool providers and is handled in accordance with their own privacy policies. We ensure that these providers adhere to stringent data protection standards.

We prioritize the security and confidentiality of your personal data. Access to collected data is restricted to authorized personnel only, and we have implemented industry-standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction of your personal information.

4. Children's Privacy

The Game is not intended for use by individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you believe that we have inadvertently collected personal data from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or the handling of your personal data, please contact us through the Apple Store or Google Play comments.

6. Changes to this Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Privacy Policy at any time. We will notify you of any material changes by updating the "Effective Date" at the beginning of this policy. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes.

By using the Game, you signify your acceptance of this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree with this Privacy Policy, please refrain from using the Game.

End of Privacy Policy
