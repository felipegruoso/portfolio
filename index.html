Who am I?

My Job

Well, the development came to my life around my 15 years old, when I used to get "locked" in my room playing MMORPGs haha.. It was when I discover the PIRATE SERVERS (yay) and I found a guy who called me to make our own. We tried, but, unfortunately, our partnership didn't last for long...

I used to develop in C# (it was the best then). Building if structures, I tought I knew everything hahaha.

In 2011 I started my graduation. It took me 4 years to receive my degree. I'm very thankful for my grandma who helped me financially on the first year, from it on I got a scholarship that certainly made me arrive where I am today.

My first job with development was as intern, in December 2012, when I met the Ruby language <3 (subsequently I was hired). From that moment to now I become a Ruby Developer.

What is development for me? Development is to exercise my mind, to solve problems, to create solutions, to work with logic, and of course, I love that.
